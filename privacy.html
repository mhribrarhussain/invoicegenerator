Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 11, 2026

InvoicePro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our free online invoice generator service at https://invtopdf.netlify.app/.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use our invoice generator, you may provide:

  • Business Information: Business name, client name, invoice details
  • Invoice Data: Item descriptions, quantities, prices, payment terms, notes
  • No Account Required: We do NOT require you to create an account or provide personal information like email addresses

Important: All invoice data you enter is processed locally in your browser. We do NOT store, collect, or transmit your invoice data to any server. Your data remains private on your device.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain information when you visit our website:

  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device to improve user experience
  • Analytics Data: Usage statistics via Google Analytics (if enabled)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Service Operation: To provide and maintain our invoice generation service
  • Improvements: To analyze usage patterns and improve our website
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our service
  • Advertising: To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google may use cookies and web beacons to collect information about your visits to this and other websites to provide relevant advertisements.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by visiting www.aboutads.info.

3.2 Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous information about how visitors use our site. This data helps us improve user experience.

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences (e.g., selected currency)
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns
  • Serve personalized advertisements

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to display relevant ads

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

5. Data Storage and Security

Client-Side Processing: Your invoice data is processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. We do NOT store or transmit your invoice information to any server.

No Database: We do not maintain a database of user invoices or personal information.

PDF Generation: PDF files are generated locally on your device using the jsPDF library. No data is sent to external servers during PDF creation.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request information about data we collect
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (Note: We don't store invoice data)
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Cookie Control: Manage or disable cookies through browser settings

9. International Users

Our service is hosted on GitHub Pages. If you access our service from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States or other countries.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@invoicepro.com

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:
