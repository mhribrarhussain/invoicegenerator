About InvoicePro

Empowering freelancers and small businesses with professional, free, and secure financial tools.

Our Mission

At InvoicePro, we believe that administrative tasks shouldn't stand in the way of your passion. Our mission is to simplify the invoicing process for freelancers, contractors, and small business owners worldwide. We provide a clean, fast, and completely free alternative to complex accounting software.

Why We Built This

As freelancers ourselves, we understand the frustration of spending hours on paperwork or paying monthly subscriptions just to send a few invoices. We wanted a tool that was:

  • Instant: No sign-up walls or lengthy onboarding.
  • Private: Data stays in the browser, ensuring 100% privacy.
  • Professional: Beautifully designed templates that make you look good.
  • Free: Accessible to everyone, regardless of budget.

The Team

InvoicePro is developed and maintained by CodeWithIbrar. We are passionate about web technologies and building useful utilities for the developer and freelancer community. We are constantly improving our tools based on user feedback.

Privacy First

We take your privacy seriously. Unlike other platforms, InvoicePro operates entirely on the client-side. This means your client data, financial details, and invoice history are never stored on our servers. When you close the tab, the data is wiped (unless you choose to save it locally). You can read more in our Privacy Policy.
